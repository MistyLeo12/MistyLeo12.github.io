Gabriel Crowell

Hey! My name is Gabriel Crowell I'm a problem solver and builder with experiences in software engineering, data science, and global health consulting.

Growing up most of my childhood in El Salvador and Texas, I became interested in the utilizing data to make real impact on marginalized communities by solving complex problems. Got hooked on the idea of programming with the old, "you can be an architect of the future" pitch. Also, it doesn't hurt that I love creating, learning new things, and helping out! Scalability excites me, performance pushes me, and most of all writing pragmatic code pleases me.

When I'm away from my keyboard you can catch me swimming, bikepacking, making music on my little OP-Z, experimenting in the kitchen, stargazing, sailing, or figuring out how to bring good coffee with me while camping.
